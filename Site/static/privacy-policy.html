Bolt Privacy Policy

Effective Date: September 29, 2015
Date of Last Update: September 29, 2015

At Bolt, LLC (“Bolt”), we understand that respecting your privacy is critical to maintaining your trust. This Privacy Policy is an important part of that commitment. It explains how we collect, use, and share the information that we collect about you when you visit our website, bolt.energy, and when you use our services (collectively, the “Site”). We will always try to keep you informed about our privacy practices and we welcome your feedback, questions, or concerns.

This Privacy Policy pertains only to information that is collected on or through the Site. Please note that by using the Site, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Policy and agree to be bound by its terms. If you do not agree to this Policy, please do not use the Site.

I. Information We Collect

When you visit the Site, we may collect either personally identifiable information, which is information that specifically identifies you, or we may collect non-personally identifiable information, which is data that does not directly identify you (collectively, “Information”). We collect Information from you in the following ways:

  1. Information you provide us voluntarily: we may collect and store information that you voluntarily give to us, such as your email address.
  2. Information we collect automatically: we may also collect and store information that is automatically collected from you when you visit our Site. This type of information may include your IP address, data that is collected via cookies, web beacons, or other technical mechanisms, and information about how you use our Site.

The above list provides an example of the information that we may collect. From time to time, we may collect information from you in other ways that are not described above..

II. Use of Information

We use information collected from you primarily for the following purposes:

  1. To send you information about Bolt, such as updates about our Site and the services we offer, as well as other news or services you may be interested in;
  2. To improve our Site by seeing which areas and features are most popular, to count the number of computers accessing our Site, to personalize and improve your experience, and to record your preferences;
  3. To track and analyze non-identifying and aggregate usage and statistical information from our visitors;
  4. To respond to questions or comments that you send to us via email;
  5. To complete a transaction or service requested by you;
  6. To administer our Site, or anticipate and resolve problems with the Site;
  7. To notify you about a material change to this Privacy Policy, if necessary.

III. Sharing and Disclosure of Information

In certain circumstances, we may share or disclose the information we collect from you. For example:

  1. We may share information with third-party service providers that help us administer, improve, or analyze our Site. These third parties may include, but are not limited to, vendors, independent contractors, service providers, and strategic partners.
  2. We may also share information with unaffiliated third parties in order to fulfill a service to you or otherwise respond to a request from you.
  3. We may also share information in order to comply with the law or in the good faith belief that such action is necessary in order to conform to the requirements of law or comply with legal process served on us, protect and defend our rights or property or act in urgent circumstances to protect the personal safety of our end users.
  4. We may share or transfer information to third parties as part of any corporate reorganization process including, but not limited to, mergers, acquisitions, or sales of all or substantially all of our assets.
  5. To handle any physical or other type of threat made to you or others, to protect property or to defend or assert legal rights.

Except as described in this Privacy Policy or at the time we request the information, we do not otherwise use, share or disclose your information to any third parties.

IV. Email Communication and Opting Out

We may send you communications or data regarding the Site, including but not limited to notices about your use of the Site and updates about our services. Some email messages may be commercial in nature, as they may advertise a feature of our Site that you may be interested in, or otherwise discuss a marketing campaign or promotion offered by Bolt or one of our partners. We give you the opportunity to opt-out of receiving these commercial emails by following the opt-out instructions provided in the message.

Bolt is not designed or intended for children under the age of 13. We do not collect personally identifiable information from any person we actually know is under the age of 13. In the event we learn that we have inadvertently collected such information, we will delete or destroy it.

No data transmissions over the Internet can be guaranteed to be 100 percent secure. Consequently, we cannot ensure or warrant the security of any information you transmit to us and you understand that any information that you transfer to us is done at your own risk.

We take all reasonable measures to ensure that your personally identifiable information remains as secure as reasonably possible. For example, only authorized employees are permitted to access your personally identifiable information. These personnel must abide by our Privacy Policy.

Here are some of the other security procedures that Bolt uses to protect your privacy:

  • We use firewalls to protect information held in our servers.
  • We utilizes Secure Socket Layer (SSL) encryption in transmitting personally identifiable information to our servers.

VII. Updating Your Information and Contacting Bolt

We welcome your feedback, questions, or concerns. To contact us about your personally identifiable information, or any other issues related to Bolt or your use of the Site, please contact us at hello@bolt.energy.

Any changes to the Policy shall be effective upon the posting of the revised Policy. We will indicate the most current date of any change and encourage you to consult our Policy from time to time. Continued use of Bolt’s Site indicates you accept such changes.
